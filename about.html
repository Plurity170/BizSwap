About BizSwap

BizSwap is a digital marketplace designed to connect people who want to buy, sell, or exchange businesses and business ideas in a simple and transparent way.

Our goal is to lower the barrier to entry for entrepreneurs by providing a structured platform where opportunities can be shared, evaluated, and explored without unnecessary complexity.

Whether you are looking to acquire an existing business, sell one you’ve built, or learn from real-world case studies, BizSwap exists to support that journey.

Our Vision

To become a trusted pan-regional platform that empowers entrepreneurs and small business owners through clarity, access, and collaboration.
