About BizSwap

A pan-regional marketplace connecting buyers and sellers across industries.

What Is BizSwap?

BizSwap is an online listing platform designed to connect individuals and businesses looking to buy, sell, or exchange business opportunities, products, and services across regions.

BizSwap does not act as a broker, agent, or intermediary in transactions. Our role is limited to providing visibility and contact between parties.

How It Works

  • Sellers submit listings for review.
  • Approved listings are published on the platform.
  • Buyers contact sellers directly via WhatsApp or provided details.
  • All negotiations and transactions occur independently.

Disclaimer

BizSwap does not handle payments, escrow, delivery, or contracts. All users are responsible for conducting their own due diligence.

BizSwap shall not be held liable for disputes, losses, or damages arising from interactions between buyers and sellers.

Users are responsible for complying with all applicable local laws and regulations in their respective jurisdictions.
