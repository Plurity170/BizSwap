Featured Business Study

Case Study: Small Retail Shop Sale

A local retail shop owner decided to sell after 8 years of operation. The business had stable monthly revenue, basic financial records, and a loyal customer base.

Key lessons from this sale:

  • Clean financial records increase buyer trust
  • Simple operations attract first-time buyers
  • Clear reasons for selling reduce negotiation friction
