Valuation Basics

Revenue vs Profit

Revenue sounds impressive. Profit pays the bills. Always value businesses on cash flow, not top-line numbers.

Owner Dependence

If the business collapses without the owner, the value is lower — sometimes much lower.

Multiples Explained

Small businesses are often valued at 2×–4× annual profit, depending on risk, stability, and systems.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

Buying With Emotion

Falling in love with a business blinds buyers to red flags. Numbers come first — always.

Rushing the Sale

Sellers often accept bad deals due to burnout or fear. Understanding options creates leverage.

Ignoring Due Diligence

Skipping proper review is the fastest way to regret. Time spent verifying saves money later.
